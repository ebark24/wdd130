Content

Home page

Site Purpose People will want to come to this website for all of their accounting needs. There are many different services available within accounting but two of the main ones are tax and auditing. Accounting is an important part of our society and no matter who you are, whether you are an individual who needs help filing their taxes or a large company that needs help with their financial statements Barker accounting has what you need. By looking at the tax page or the auditing page they can have their questions answered about the types of services that we provide and what working with our company will look like. The main page will have our values and help to show that we are different from our competitors. It will have a header with the logo and well as tabs for the other parts of the website. As customers scroll down they will see our mission statement and a little things about why we value our customers. It will show some pictures relating to accounting. The mission statement will be something like the following: Our team here at Barker accounting is special because we truly care about our customers and ensure that they have an enjoyable experience with all their accounting needs. Our team of professional CPAs are here to help you and make sure that all of your needs are met. Whether you are here for tax or audit you are sure to have an experience that you will never forget and will have you coming back for more! Underneath this it will have two large background pictures with text and a link on them. These two parts will give a brief description of the tax services and the audit services and the link will bring them to the other child pages where there is more information. At the bottom of the page will be a footer with links to social media as well as a link to contact us.

Images for the Home page

Tax

One of the two pages will be for tax. It will have the same header and footer as the main page. The actual text of the page will be something like the following: Everyone has to file their taxes and it can often be tricky to know how exactly to file them correctly and how to receive the greatest amount of savings when filing taxes. From your 1040s and your W2s to your schedule Cs we cover everything tax related. Make your tax experience an effortless and enjoyable experience. Our tax experts will help you to identify the proper forms and schedules to fill out and will ensure that you receive the deductions and credits that you deserve. You can contact one of our tax professionals by calling us or by sending us an email. Under this there will be a dropdown box for people to see answers to tax questions and to see the fact that both individuals and businesses can receive services from the website.

Images for the Page 2

Audit

The other child page will be for audit services. It will have the same header and footer and will have text and something along these lines: Whether you are a public or a nonpublic company it is a necessity to have your financial statements audited and ensure that you have appropriate controls in place for your business. Our fast and friendly team of auditors will ensure that your audit experience is one that is conducive to your professional environment and provides the services that you need. If you have questions about our audit team or would like to talk to one of our head staff auditors please contact us below by sending an email or giving us a call!
